House Rules

Contact & Book

To ensure all guests enjoy a comfortable and relaxing stay, we kindly ask everyone to observe the following house rules. Thank you for your cooperation.

🕓 Check-in & Check-out

  • Check-in: from 16:00
  • Check-out: by 10:00
  • Luggage drop-off is available upon request — please let us know in advance.

🏊 The Pool

  • The pool is available from mid-June until the end of August.
  • The pool and gardens are for guest use only.
  • Pool hours: 09:00 – 22:00.
  • The pool is cleaned and maintained at certain times. When maintenance is in progress please refrain from using the pool — it normally takes no longer than 30 minutes.
  • Water bed: please do not jump onto the water bed as it may burst.
  • No glassware is permitted in the pool area for safety reasons. Plastic ware is available in the kitchen.

🛏 Rooms

  • Each room has a ceiling fan — please switch it off when you leave the room.
  • No food to be consumed in the rooms as this attracts ants.
  • Each room has a locking door for your privacy and security.

🍳 Kitchen

  • All items used should be washed, dried and put away after use.
  • The hob is available for guests to use. The oven is not available.

🚿 Bathrooms

  • Please leave the bathrooms as you would like to find them. There should always be soap, shower gel, shampoo and toilet paper available. If any of these run out, please inform the hosts and they will be replenished promptly.

🌿 The Garden

  • The garden is available throughout the day and is a shared resource. We ask that you respect other guests and our neighbours by keeping noise to an acceptable level at all times.

🍽 Indoor Dining Area

  • The indoor dining area is available when weather prevents outdoor dining.

🧊 Hallway Refrigerator

  • The hallway refrigerator is available for all guests. A dedicated shelf will be assigned to your room. The remaining shared space is available for general use.

♻️ Waste

  • There is a waste basket in the hallway for plastic bottles only. Please use the appropriate bins for all other waste.

🗺 Tourist Information

  • We provide a selection of tourist information pamphlets and books. Feel free to photograph and use this information. Please leave the books and leaflets on the table for other guests. Copies can also be obtained at the tourist information kiosk in Tamariu.

🧺 Washing Facilities

  • A washing machine is available at a cost of €5 per load, including washing powder.
  • Drying facilities are available at the side of the house.

👔 Ironing Facilities

  • Ironing facilities are available on request — please ask the hosts.

🌙 Returning Late

  • You are welcome to stay out as late as you like. When you return, please respect other guests and the hosts by keeping noise to a minimum.
