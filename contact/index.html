Cancellation Policy

We understand that plans can change — here's how we handle cancellations.

✅ Cancel 7 days or more before your stay and we'll refund your payment in full — no questions asked.

We want you to feel confident when you book with us. If something comes up and you need to cancel, simply send us an email at tamariuchalet@gmail.com at least 7 days before your arrival date, and we will arrange a complete refund of any payment made.

For cancellations made within 7 days of arrival, we are unfortunately unable to offer a refund, as the accommodation will have been reserved exclusively for you. However, we will always try to work with you if exceptional circumstances arise — so please don't hesitate to get in touch.

To cancel, please email us with your name, booking dates, and accommodation. We aim to process all refunds within 5 working days.
