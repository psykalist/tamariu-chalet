★★★★★

My friend and I recently spent a week at Kieran and Ceci’s place in Tamariu. It was our first time in Costa Brava and we couldn’t be happier with our choice of stay! Both hosts were very kind, communicated thoroughly and offered us tips to get to know the area.
The place itself is lovely, with a totally private entrance to the house. It had all you needed - from towels/linens to kitchen utensils and basic cooking ingredients. It was clear that a lot of care was put into the organization of the space. Their house has a great backyard with a pool as well.
Lastly, Tamariu is a great little town with incredible beaches and small restaurants. It was very convenient to drive around other villages in the area, and easy to find parking pretty much anywhere.
Would absolutely recommend to anyone looking for a calm stay in Costa Brava. Thank you Kieran and Ceci for a great stay!
