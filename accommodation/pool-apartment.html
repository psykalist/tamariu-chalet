Studio Apartment

About the Apartment

Our fully self-contained Studio Apartment offers the ultimate in privacy and independence. With its own private entrance, the apartment sits directly alongside the pool and garden, making it perfect for couples or small families who want the freedom of a home from home combined with the surroundings of Tamariu Chalet.

The apartment features a comfortable bedroom, a well-equipped private kitchen, a sitting area and its own bathroom — everything you need for a relaxed, independent stay on the Costa Brava. Step outside onto your terrace to the sight and sound of the pool, with the garden beyond. Free on-street parking is available just outside, and secure bike storage is provided at the chalet.
