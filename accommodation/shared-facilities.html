Accommodation → Shared Facilities

Shared Bathrooms & Kitchen

Guests in the Double Room, Double/Twin Room 1 and Double/Twin Room 2 share two well-equipped bathrooms and a fully fitted shared kitchen. All facilities are kept to a high standard of cleanliness and are comfortably sized for the number of rooms they serve.

Each bathroom serves no more than two rooms. The shared kitchen is fully equipped with everything you need for self-catering — from a full hob to coffee maker, toaster and all crockery.

The Bathrooms

Bathroom 1

A clean, well-appointed bathroom with walk-in shower, wash hand basin, toilet and all necessary toiletry fittings. Towels provided.

Bathroom 2

A second bright bathroom with shower, wash hand basin and toilet. Kept freshly laundered and stocked for each set of guests.

The Kitchen

Shared Kitchen

Fully equipped with hob, microwave, kettle, toaster, coffee maker, full crockery and cutlery, glasses, pots and pans. Everything you need for a relaxed self-catering stay.

Shared Kitchen

Another view of the shared kitchen, showing the full counter space, storage and cooking facilities available to all guests in the main house rooms.
