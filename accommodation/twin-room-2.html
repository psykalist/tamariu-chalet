About This Room

A relaxed, comfortable twin room with two single beds and a warm Mediterranean feel. Natural light fills the space throughout the day, and the garden outlook lends a sense of calm privacy. The room benefits from quality bed linen, ample storage and convenient access to the shared bathroom facilities just across the hall.
