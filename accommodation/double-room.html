About This Room

A comfortable, well-appointed double room with a relaxed Mediterranean character. Natural light fills the space throughout the day, and the garden outlook lends a sense of calm privacy. The room benefits from quality bed linen, ample storage and convenient access to the shared bathroom facilities just across the hall.
