Local Restaurants

The Costa Brava has a remarkable restaurant scene, from simple beach chiringuitos serving grilled fish to creative modern Catalan restaurants. Here are our personal recommendations in and around Tamariu.

In Tamariu

Restaurant Tamariu

The village's most established restaurant, right on the beach. Classic Catalan seafood — fresh fish, fideuà (the Catalan version of paella with noodles), grilled prawns and excellent local wine. Booking essential in summer.

La Sirena

A relaxed beachside terrace serving fresh seafood, salads and simple grills. Perfect for a long lazy lunch watching the boats. Very popular — arrive early or book ahead.

Nearby — Llafranc & Calella de Palafrugell

El Far Hotel-Restaurant, Llafranc

Perched on the lighthouse headland above Llafranc with extraordinary sea views. Creative modern Catalan cooking using the best local ingredients. A special occasion restaurant.

La Malcontenta, Calella de Palafrugell

A beloved Calella institution — simple, honest Catalan cooking in a colourful setting. The rice dishes are outstanding. Long summer lunches here are a highlight of the Costa Brava experience.

Palafrugell Town

La Xicra

One of the most acclaimed restaurants in the area — sophisticated Catalan cuisine in an intimate setting. The tasting menu is excellent value for the quality on offer. Book well in advance.

Mas Pou

A classic Catalan country restaurant outside Palafrugell, in a converted farmhouse with a large garden terrace. Outstanding grilled meats, excellent local wine list, very good value.

Further Afield

Hotel Restaurant Aigua Blava, Begur

A legendary Costa Brava institution overlooking a perfect cove near Begur. The setting is unbeatable and the traditional Catalan cooking is consistently excellent.
