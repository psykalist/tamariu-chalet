Palafrugell Tourist Information

The Palafrugell Tourist Information Office is an excellent resource for planning your time in the area — with up-to-date information on events, activities, transport and local attractions.

Visit Palafrugell — Official Tourism Website

The official tourism portal for Palafrugell, covering Tamariu, Llafranc, Calella de Palafrugell and the wider municipality. Events, beaches, activities and practical information.

→

Tourist Office — Palafrugell

The main tourist office is located in Palafrugell town centre. The team can provide maps, event listings, bus timetables and personal recommendations. English is spoken.

  • Open Monday–Saturday in season; reduced hours off-season
  • Free maps of the coastal path and cycle routes available
  • Current events listings including concerts, markets and festivals
  • Habanera song festival information (a famous annual event in Calella de Palafrugell)

Local Bus Services

Two seasonal bus services make it easy to explore the area without a car:

Julivia Bus

A seasonal tourist bus linking Palafrugell with the coastal villages of Tamariu, Llafranc and Calella de Palafrugell. Runs approximately May to October — affordable, pay on board.

→

Begur Beach Bus

A shuttle service connecting Begur town with its surrounding coves and beaches — ideal for accessing the more remote spots without needing a car or worrying about parking.

→

Useful Local Information

  • Tamariu has a small supermarket open in season; Palafrugell has larger supermarkets including Mercadona and Lidl
  • The nearest pharmacy is in Palafrugell town centre
  • The nearest hospital is in Palamós (Hospital de Palamós), 15 minutes by car
  • Petrol stations are in Palafrugell; none in Tamariu itself
  • Parking in Tamariu is limited in peak summer — early arrival recommended
