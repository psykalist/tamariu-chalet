Walking in the Area

The coastline and hinterland around Tamariu offer superb walking for all abilities — from gentle strolls between coves to longer hikes through the cork oak forests of the Gavarres. The famous GR-92 coastal path passes right through the village.

Walking Tips

  • The coastal path can be rocky and uneven — proper walking shoes are recommended
  • Carry water — there are few water sources on the coastal path
  • Start early in summer to avoid the heat — paths are shaded in the Gavarres but fully exposed on the coast
  • The GR-92 is marked with red and white paint blazes on rocks and trees
  • Several coves along the way are swimmable — pack a towel and snorkel
