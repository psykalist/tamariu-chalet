Getting to the Beach

The beach is just 750 metres from the house — a short, easy walk down through the quiet residential streets of the village. There are some steps along the way, and it takes only a few minutes on foot.

At a Glance

  • Distance: about 750 metres from the house
  • On foot: just a few minutes, mostly downhill
  • Route: quiet residential streets with some steps

Directions to the Beach

  1. When leaving the property, turn left and walk to the end of the road.
  2. Just past number 19 you'll find a set of stairs. Go down these, and at the bottom turn right.
  3. Follow the road along and then down a steep section. Where the road bears right, there is another set of steps on the left.
  4. Go down these steps and, at the bottom, turn right. In about 20 metres you'll come to a set of steps that lead up to the top of the village.
  5. Follow the road as it bears left — the larger Spar supermarket will be in front of you, across the main road.
  6. Take any left-hand road from here and it will lead you down to the beach.
