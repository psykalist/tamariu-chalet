Getting to Tamariu Chalet

Find Us

We're located in the village of Tamariu, within the municipality of Palafrugell, on the Costa Brava coast of Catalunya, Spain.

📍 Open in Google Maps

By Car

From Barcelona (approx. 2 hrs)

Take the AP-7 motorway north towards Girona. Exit at junction 9 (Palamós / Palafrugell). Follow the C-31 towards Palafrugell, then the GI-660 to Tamariu. The village is signposted from Palafrugell.

From Girona Airport (approx. 50 mins)

Exit the airport and take the C-65 south towards La Bisbal d'Empordà, then continue on the C-31 to Palafrugell, and the GI-660 to Tamariu. Car hire is available at the airport.

From Barcelona Airport (approx. 2.5 hrs)

Take the AP-7 motorway north. Follow the same route as from Barcelona city centre — approximately 2.5 hours depending on traffic. Car hire is available at the airport.

By Train + Bus

Train to Girona or Flaçà

High-speed trains (AVE) run from Barcelona Sants to Girona in approximately 40 minutes. From Girona station, take a Sarfa bus to Palafrugell (approx. 1 hour), then a local bus or taxi to Tamariu (10 minutes). Alternatively, regional trains stop at Flaçà, which has a less frequent but direct bus to Palafrugell.

Sarfa Bus (Palafrugell ↔ Tamariu)

The Sarfa bus company runs a seasonal service from Palafrugell bus station to Tamariu, Llafranc and Calella de Palafrugell. Services are more frequent in July and August. Check current timetables at sarfa.com.

By Air

  • Girona–Costa Brava Airport — 50 km (recommended for short-haul European flights)
  • Barcelona El Prat Airport — 130 km (most international connections)
  • Reus Airport — 130 km (Ryanair hub; some European routes)
