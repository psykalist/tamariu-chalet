The Julivia Bus

The Julivia Bus is a seasonal tourist bus service connecting the coastal villages and inland towns of the Palafrugell area — a convenient and enjoyable way to explore the region without a car.

Julivia Bus — Official Route & Timetable Information

Full route information, timetables, stops and prices on the official Visit Palafrugell website.

→

About the Service

The Julivia Bus links the inland town of Palafrugell with the coastal villages of Tamariu, Llafranc and Calella de Palafrugell — making it easy to get between the coast and town without worrying about parking. It also serves some inland cultural and historical sites.

  • Seasonal service — typically operates May to October
  • Affordable tickets — pay on board
  • Connects Tamariu directly to Palafrugell town centre (for markets, shops, restaurants)
  • Allows easy exploration of all three coastal villages by public transport
  • Check the official website above for current year timetables and route map

Combined with the Sarfa regional bus network, the Julivia Bus makes it perfectly feasible to explore the area without a car during the main season.
