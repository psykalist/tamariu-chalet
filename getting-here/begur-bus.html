Begur Beach Bus

The Begur Beach Bus (Bus Platges Begur) is a seasonal shuttle service connecting the hilltop town of Begur with its string of beautiful cove beaches — a superb way to explore some of the most spectacular coastline on the Costa Brava.

About the Service

Begur is a gorgeous hilltop castle town about 10 km north of Tamariu. Its beaches are among the most beautiful on the Costa Brava — several are only accessible by the beach bus or on foot, keeping them delightfully uncrowded even in peak summer.

Beaches Served

  • Sa Riera — A pretty sheltered cove with a small beach and excellent restaurants
  • Aiguafreda — Tiny, intimate cove — one of the most beautiful on the coast
  • Sa Tuna — A picturesque fishing village cove with crystal-clear water
  • Fornells — A larger bay with a sandy beach and water sports hire
  • Platja de Pals — A longer sandy beach backed by pine trees

Tips for Using the Bus

  • The service runs July and August daily; reduced schedule in June and September
  • Departs from Begur town centre; check current PDF timetable for times
  • A great way to beach-hop — take the bus down and walk back (or vice versa) along the coastal path
  • From Tamariu: drive or take the Julivia Bus to Palafrugell, then connect to Begur by car or taxi (10 mins)
