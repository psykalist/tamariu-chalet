Getting to the Beach

A 3 km circular walk from Tamariu Chalet down to the beach and back

The walk from Tamariu Chalet is one of the highlights of any stay. The route winds along the coast before dropping down to the Aigua Xelida beach — one of the most remote beaches on the Costa Brava.

The loop shown below follows the 3 km circular route, so you can return a different way and see more of the village. The path is well-shaded and suitable for all fitness levels, though there are some steps on the descent to the beach.

Walk Notes

  • Start from the chalet and head downhill through the village lanes towards the coast.
  • The route loops around, giving you views over the bay and the surrounding pine forests.
  • Tamariu beach has a handful of excellent restaurants and beach bars right on the sand — perfect for lunch.
  • The return leg climbs gently back up through the village. Take your time — the views are worth it.
  • Wear comfortable shoes; the path includes some uneven surfaces and steps near the beach.
  • In summer, the beach gets busy by mid-morning — aim to arrive early for the best spot.
